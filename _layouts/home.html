In a Nutshell

Place2Be is a Service that integrates in background of your existing front-ends (apps, websites, ...)

  • You define Gamification Schemes and receive API keys.

    Our Doc contains Tutorials to help you getting started, or you can even start from a template.

  • Your front-ends connect to P2B and report player actions.
  • Players are instantly notified of the progress they make accross the Schemes.
  • Aggregated analytics are sent in realtime to connected players to monitor (other) players progress.

    You decide what is aggregated and what is sent to players. See Live Metrics Doc

  • Large scale Gamification Schemes are supported. Up to hundreds of thousands of Players concurrently, in teams or as individuals, from simple to complex mechanics.
How does it work?

Configuration via GUI - Interaction via API

  1. Scheme creation and configuration are done via Place2Be.io website
  2. New or Updated schemes are instantly available in the could.
    • No propagation delay
    • No programming skills needed
  3. Clients connect to it via websockets.
    • Plain standard, no extra library needed
    • No upper bound on requests per period of time
